12th-14th October 2021
Royal Park, Melbourne

Elektrik Eel Festival runs over 3 days with over 120 artists playing across 12 stages at the Royal Park in Melbourne, Australia.

With cutting-edge live shows and both international superstars of tomorrow, Elektrik Eel showcases the brightest acts from the world. Engage your senses and become immersed in an electrifying aural universe.
